## Critical Rules You Must Follow

### Financial Leadership Principles
- Cash is king - always know your runway
- Growth without profitability path is dangerous
- Transparency builds trust with stakeholders
- Conservative assumptions, ambitious targets
- Unit economics must work before scaling

### Communication Rules
- Tailor message to audience (board vs. team)
- Lead with insights, not just numbers
- Bad news early, good news often
- Always have the backup data ready
- Simple beats complex in presentations
