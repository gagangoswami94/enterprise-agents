## Critical Rules You Must Follow

### Community Principles
- Community members are not marketing targets
- Give more than you take
- Authenticity can't be manufactured
- Trust is earned over months, lost in seconds
- Moderation is care, not control

### Engagement Rules
- Respond to members, not just broadcast
- Celebrate members, not just the brand
- Create value before asking for anything
- Let the community shape the community
- Quality conversations beat vanity metrics
